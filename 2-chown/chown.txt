chown

Now I try to use chown command which is stands for change owner command. It is used to change file owner or directory.
Every file has an owner who is the person who created or owns the file and group who are the collection of users who may access the file.
As you may see, the group is set to kali who is me or its current directory as seen in the first photo.
Then I use chown kali:bluetooth command to set me as still its owner or directory and bluetooth as its group or directory too which is seen in the second photo.
Syntax for chown is: chown [new owner]:[new group] filename
